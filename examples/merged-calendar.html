Merged Ethiopian Calendar

About Merged View

The merged calendar view displays both Ethiopian and Gregorian dates in a single calendar grid. You can choose which calendar is primary (takes most space) and which is secondary (shows in the corner).

  • Primary calendar: Date displayed prominently in the center
  • Secondary calendar: Date displayed in the top-right corner
  • Toggle between Ethiopian and Gregorian as primary using the checkbox
